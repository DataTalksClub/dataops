### Event Description

Title: Thriving in the AI Era with Human Skills

Subtitle: Building a Future-Ready Data Career with Maryam Ramezani-Bartsch

As companies eagerly adopt the latest AI tools, many overlook the foundational data strategy required to actually make them work. Maryam has seen firsthand how the rapid evolution of technology is blurring the lines between traditional data roles. In this new landscape, professionals are increasingly expected to be AI-empowered generalists, yet maintaining a strong core technical competency is more important than ever to stand out.

However, technical skills alone are no longer enough to guarantee long-term success. In this episode, Maryam shares her deeply personal career journey from battling perfectionism and navigating burnout to learning how to fail forward and manage complex stakeholder alignment. She reveals why the top three human skills are the ultimate career moats in an automated world. Listeners will walk away with actionable tools, including insights from her Design Thinking workshops, to help them intentionally craft and future-proof their dream data careers.

She’ll cover:

What AI Changes about Data Careers

The Human Skills That Become More Valuable as AI Advances

Designing Your Next Career Chapter Intentionally

About the Speaker:

Maryam Ramezani-Bartsch is Data and AI leader with 20+ years experience leading data teams across big corporates such as adidas, sky, delivery hero and Zalando. Find out more at maryamramezani.com.
