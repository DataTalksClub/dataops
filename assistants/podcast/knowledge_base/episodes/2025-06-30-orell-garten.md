Title: From Simulation Algorithms to Production-Grade Data Systems

Subtitle: Leveraging Simulation Expertise for Rapid Prototyping, Automation, and Scalable Data Pipelines - Orell Garten

Description:

Join us for an in-depth conversation with Orell Garten, a former simulation engineer turned freelance data consultant. He’ll share how his background in high-precision electromagnetic simulations led to today’s rapid-prototyping, automation, and production-grade data pipelines.

What we’ll cover:

Simulation Foundations: How simulations inform the way Orell builds reliable data pipelines.

Rapid MVPs: The one-month prototype rule and how to apply it in your work.

Production Checklist: The must-do steps (CI/CD, monitoring, schema versioning) and the common blind spots teams miss.

Workflow Automation: Orell’s quick rule for spotting repeatable tasks, measuring ROI, and a real example of saved engineering hours.

Orell’s disciplined, practical approach will give you clear, actionable steps to move from concept to production. Register now and bring your questions!

About Our Guest, Orell Garten

Orell Garten is a freelance IT consultant and software engineer based in Leipzig, Germany. He began his career building high-precision simulation pipelines at TU Dresden and Ruhr-Universität Bochum. After pausing his PhD when COVID hit, he joined a government-backed startup program to validate research prototypes in the market.

Today, Orell helps businesses worldwide by:

Custom software when off-the-shelf tools fall short

Data engineering: automated, reliable pipelines for analytics, dashboards, and ML

Workflow automation: eliminating error-prone tasks and cutting operational risk

Rapid prototyping: backend/data proofs-of-concept in four weeks

Scaling to production: from CI/CD and monitoring to full-scale, reliable systems
