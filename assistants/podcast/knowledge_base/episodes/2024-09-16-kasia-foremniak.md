Katarzyna Foremniak

Katarzyna Foremniak is a computational linguist with a Ph.D. in Contrastive Linguistics and over ten years of academic and industry experience across three main areas:

· NLP and speech recognition sector for automotive industry, educational institutions and (choosing human-centred AI approach to prioritise human needs and values)

· academic (corpus linguistics; speech analysis; morphosyntactic analysis)

· translation and localization industry (Italian, Polish, English), specialised in arts and cultural sector.

Katarzyna is also the author of the Italian grammar course for Polish speakers, Włoski w tłumaczeniach (Preston Publishing, 2015–2019), which was adapted for the German market as Satz für Satz Italienisch (PONS, 2019).

Currently she works as a researcher and academic teacher at the Faculty of Modern Languages at the University of Warsaw and collaborates with several data companies on NLP projects.
