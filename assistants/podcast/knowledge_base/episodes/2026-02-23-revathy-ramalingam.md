### Event Description

Title: How to Become an AI Engineer After a Career Break

Restarting a tech career after a long break can feel overwhelming, especially in a fast-moving field like AI. In this session, we’ll talk openly about what it really takes to return to work after years away and successfully transition into an AI Engineer role. This is not a motivational talk. It is a practical discussion grounded in real experience, real interviews, and real hiring processes.

In this conversation, we will cover:

How to restart your career after a long break

How to position past experience when switching domains

What the AI Engineer role looks like in practice

What technical questions are asked in AI Engineer interviews

What companies evaluate in take-home assignments

Practical steps to build confidence and become job-ready

If you are returning after maternity leave, changing career paths, or aiming for your first AI Engineering role, this session will give you a realistic view of the process and concrete actions you can take next.

About the guest:

Revathy Ramalingam is an AI Engineer at a healthcare startup with over nine years of prior experience in software development in the telecom domain. After a seven-year career break, she upskilled through ML Zoomcamp, AI Dev Tools Zoomcamp, and the AI Hero email course at DataTalks.Club, and successfully transitioned back into the industry. She will share the strategies, interview insights, and lessons that helped her rebuild her career step by step.
