Title: Understanding the AI Engineer Role

What does the AI engineer role look like in practice?

In this conversation, we explore the role through the career path of Nasser Qadri, AI Engineer at Google.

Nasser has an unusual path into the field. He started with a PhD in Politics and International Relations, then moved into data science roles at Gallup, Deloitte, and Quest Diagnostics, before transitioning into AI engineering.

Using his experience, we discuss how the AI engineer role appears in real projects and how people can move into it from different backgrounds.

In this conversation, we cover:

What the “AI engineer” role looks like in practice

Typical tasks and responsibilities in the role

How the role differs from ML engineer, data scientist, and software engineer

Skills that are useful for working with modern AI systems

Nasser’s transition from social science to data science and AI engineering
