# ✅ Post. Podcast. Guest recommendations

<!-- sop-section-start: summary -->
## Summary

- Purpose: Share podcast guest recommendations on LinkedIn and Twitter/X.
- Outcome: Recommendation visuals and copy are posted or prepared for social media.
- Trigger: A podcast guest shares resource recommendations.
- Frequency: After podcast episodes with guest recommendations.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

<!-- sop-section-start: prerequisites -->
## Prerequisites


- Access: Figma template and social media accounts.
- Tools: Figma, PDF/image conversion, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X.
- Inputs: Speaker name, recommendation content, Figma visuals, and post copy.
<!-- sop-section-end -->
