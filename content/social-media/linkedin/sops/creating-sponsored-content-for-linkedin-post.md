# Creating Sponsored Content for LinkedIn Post

<!-- sop-section-start: summary -->
## Summary

- Purpose: Create a sponsored LinkedIn post from newsletter sponsor materials.
- Outcome: A LinkedIn sponsored post is drafted or scheduled with text, link, and image.
- Trigger: Sponsored newsletter promotional content needs a LinkedIn post.
- Frequency: For each sponsored LinkedIn promotion.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

<!-- sop-section-start: prerequisites -->
## Prerequisites


- Access: DataTalks.Club LinkedIn page and Trello sponsorship card.
- Tools: LinkedIn and Trello.
- Inputs: Sponsorship document, LinkedIn post template, registration link, and banner image.
<!-- sop-section-end -->
