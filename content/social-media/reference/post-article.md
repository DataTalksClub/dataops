# Post. Article

## Summary
- Purpose: Share article content on LinkedIn and Twitter/X.
- Outcome: Article visuals and post text are prepared for social posting.
- Trigger: An article needs to be promoted on social media.
- Frequency: As needed when articles are published or promoted.
## Prerequisites

- Access: Figma template and social media accounts.
