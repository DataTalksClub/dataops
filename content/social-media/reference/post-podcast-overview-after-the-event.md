# ✅ Post. Podcast. Overview after the event

## Summary
- Purpose: Share a podcast overview after the event on LinkedIn and Twitter/X.
- Outcome: Podcast overview visuals and text are prepared for social posting.
- Trigger: A podcast event has finished and overview content is ready.
- Frequency: After podcast events.
## Prerequisites

- Access: Figma template, newsletter text, and social media accounts.
- Tools: Figma, PDF/image conversion, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X.
- Inputs: Podcast overview text, speaker details, and Figma carousel visuals.
