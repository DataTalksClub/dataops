## Template

Subject: BOOK_TITLE — Book of the Week at DataTalks.Club

Email body:

Hi, XXX

I’m (Your Name), nice to e-meet you! I’m taking care of organizing events at DataTalks.Club, including the book of the week event.

If you don’t know much about this event yet, you can read more about it here: [http://bit.ly/dtc-botw](http://bit.ly/dtc-botw).

In summary, we invite book authors to our Slack and ask them questions about their book. You’ll need to show up in our Slack for four days and answer questions at the time that’s most convenient for you. It should not require more than 15-20 minutes each day.

Will the week from MM D to MM D work for you?

Let me know if you are available.

I’m also adding Alexey, the founder of DataTalks.Club, who can help me answer some of your questions.

Thanks,

(Your Name)
