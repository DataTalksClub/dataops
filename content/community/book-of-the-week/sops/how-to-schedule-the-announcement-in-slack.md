# How to schedule the announcement in Slack

<!-- sop-section-start: summary -->
## Summary

- Purpose: Schedule the Book of the Week announcement in Slack.
- Outcome: The announcement is scheduled in `#book-of-the-week` and forwarded to `#announcements`.
- Trigger: A Book of the Week event is ready to announce.
- Frequency: Weekly for Book of the Week events.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

<!-- sop-section-start: prerequisites -->
## Prerequisites


- Access: Slack workspace with permission to schedule and forward messages.
- Tools: Slack, Book of the Week announcement template.
- Inputs: Book title, author name, event discussion details, and book cover image.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

## Procedure

How to schedule the announcement in Slack

4.  And then choose the picture of the book and click "Open"

5.  Once the picture of the book is attached, hover and click the dropdown button and click “Custom time”

6.  After, select the scheduled date of the book to be announced and click "Schedule message"

7.  After posting in the \#book-of-the-week, repost it to \#announcements.
    Hover over the message you sent until icons appear on the right, then click the ‘Forward message’ icon.

8.  Forward the message to \#announcements and use the following text:

    “Join \#book-of-the-week and ask @author anything!”

    Then click on the “Forward” button.
