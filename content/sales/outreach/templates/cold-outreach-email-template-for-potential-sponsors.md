## Template

Subject: Hi from DataTalks.Club

Message:

Hi \<NAME\>!

I’m Alexey and I’m running DataTalks.Club - a community of people who love data. We have 60k+ community members in Slack and 100+ newsletter subscribers.

I was wondering if \<NAME OF THE COMPANY\> would be interested in working with us. I’m subscribed to your newsletter and get notifications about your events. We can help you promote it in our community which are quite interested in MLOps-related content.

\<ADD MESSAGE, CONFERENCE, OR EVENTS THAT THEY WANT TO BE INTERESTED TO PROMOTE\>

Please let me know if it’s something interesting and if you need more information.

Example:
Hi Jess, I just came across your announcement about the anaconda learning platform! That's really great!

I'm Alexey and I'm running DataTalks.Club - a community of people who love data. We have 22k+ members in slack and a newsletter with 45k+ subscribers. We're serving the same audiences and I was wondering if you maybe would be interested in discussing a potential partnership between Anaconda and DataTalks.Club?
