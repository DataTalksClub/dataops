## Template

For people I'm not connected with:
Hi, \<NAME\>! I’m Alexey and I run DataTalks.Club - a community of people who love data. We have events like webinars and podcasts and we’re looking for speakers. Would you be interested in taking part in our events?

For people I'm already connected with:

Hi, \<NAME\>!

Maybe you know it already, but I’m running DataTalks.Club - a community of people who love data. We have events like webinars and podcasts.

Here’s more information about the events: [https://docs.google.com/document/d/e/2PACX-1vQpU6Hk6rPxzrrPIWsooYo4LCrp655C8thGb57B9V0AHo0RQszPML8j3V6zUP4XYdD8Xyk-04gp9PoV/pub](https://docs.google.com/document/d/e/2PACX-1vQpU6Hk6rPxzrrPIWsooYo4LCrp655C8thGb57B9V0AHo0RQszPML8j3V6zUP4XYdD8Xyk-04gp9PoV/pub)

I was wondering if you’d be interested to be a speaker at one of our events and share your experience with us?

From recommendation:

Hi \<NAME\>! I’m Alexey and I run DataTalks.Club - a community of people who love data. We have weekly events and we’re looking for speakers. \<REFERRAL NAME\> recommended reaching out to you. Would you be interested in taking part? Please let me know if you’d like to know more.
