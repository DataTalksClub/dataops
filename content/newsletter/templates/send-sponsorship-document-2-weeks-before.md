# Send sponsorship document 2 weeks before

## Usage

- Use when:
- Audience:
- Required inputs:

## Template

Use the appropriate subject title for the type of Newsletter:
1.  Main Slot
\<COMPANY\> + DataTalks.Club Newsletter - MM-DD-YYYY

2.  Secondary Slot
\<COMPANY\> + DataTalks.Club Newsletter (Secondary Slot) - MM-DD-YYYY

3.  Stand-alone Newsletter
\<COMPANY\> + DataTalks.Club Newsletter (Stand-alone) - MM-DD-YYYY

Template 1 : Initial Introduction and Selecting a Date for Booking with Sponsor
Subject: \<COMPANY\> + DataTalks.Club Newsletter
Hi \<NAME\>,

Nice to meet you! I'm \<Name\> and I'm a community manager at DataTalks.Club.

Based on our calendar, we have 1 available slot in \<MONTH\>.

\<MMMM DD, YYYY\>. Would you like to book on that date?

Please let me know. Thank you!

---

Template 2: Initial Introduction to Sponsor (Scheduled Newsletter Date)
- Send an initial email to introduce yourself to the sponsor and inform them of the scheduled newsletter date. Include the document for their filing and add Valeriia to review and edit as needed.

- CC Valeria in the email thread [valeriia@datatalks.club](mailto:valeriia@datatalks.club)

- The deadline should be at least 10 days before the newsletter slot date.

Example: Newsletter date is on June 16, so the deadline for receiving the copy should be June 06.

Hi \<NAME\>,

Nice to meet you! I’m Anj, a community manager at DataTalks.Club.

I wanted to remind you that \<COMPANY NAME\>’s next sponsored slot in our newsletter is scheduled for \<INSERT DATE\>, just two weeks from now.

I’ve created a Google document for you to enter your copy: \<LINK TO DOC\>

You’ll find guidelines at the top of the document and in this [visual guide](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1M_jEzcb1IWJmolx9N7e08gfdbctjcLH2/view?usp=sharing).

For inspiration, here are a few examples of past sponsored content from our newsletter:

- "Model Monitoring Industry Certification": [https://mailchi.mp/datatalks.club/weekly-108](https://mailchi.mp/datatalks.club/weekly-108)

- "The Ultimate Guide to Scaling AI: AI at Scale": [https://mailchi.mp/datatalks.club/weekly-110](https://mailchi.mp/datatalks.club/weekly-110)

- "The Robust & Responsible AI Summit 2023": [https://mailchi.mp/datatalks.club/weekly-106](https://mailchi.mp/datatalks.club/weekly-106)

You can find more examples [here](https://us19.campaign-archive.com/home/?u=0d7822ab98152f5afc118c176&id=97178021aa).

To ensure we have enough time for any adjustments, please submit your copy and graphics by \<DEADLINE\>.

I have also added Valeriia who will review the document.

Looking forward to receiving your materials!

Thanks!
