# Filling Newsletter Statistics

<!-- sop-section-start: summary -->
## Summary

- Purpose: Newsletter Statistics refers to the data concerning the amount of “clicks” and “impressions” of a sponsored link: Mailchimp, LinkedIn and X.
- Outcome: Filling in Newsletter Statistics is important to update our sponsors how well their newsletters perform and to ensure that we track the success of sponsored links accurately.
- Trigger: It is a weekly update (Monday).
- Frequency: Weekly, on Monday.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

<!-- sop-section-start: prerequisites -->
## Prerequisites


- Access: DataTalks.Club schedule spreadsheet, Mailchimp reports, LinkedIn analytics, and X analytics.
- Tools: Google Sheets, Mailchimp, LinkedIn, and X.
- Inputs: Sponsor name, newsletter number, sponsored link, and social media posts.
<!-- sop-section-end -->
