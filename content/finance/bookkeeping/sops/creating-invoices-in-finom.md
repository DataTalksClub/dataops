# Creating Invoices in Finom

<!-- sop-section-start: summary -->
## Summary

- Purpose: Create sponsor invoices in Finom.
- Outcome: A Finom invoice is created with the correct sponsor, items, tax, and bank details.
- Trigger: A sponsor or client needs an invoice.
- Frequency: As needed
<!-- sop-section-end -->

<!-- sop-section-start: prerequisites -->
## Prerequisites


- Access: Finom and sponsor billing details.
- Tools: Finom.
- Inputs: Sponsor name, billing address, VAT ID if applicable, invoice items, amount, dates, and bank details.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

11. Next, select the right VAT.

    The rules are as follows:

    - For Germany, it's 19% (follow steps 12-A).
    - For other EU countries, it's 0% with the note "VAT 0%: reverse charge" (follow steps on 12-B).
    - For other countries, it's 0% and the note is not needed (follow steps 12-C).

    12-A. If the client is in Germany:

    Add a 19 VAT (%). No need to for “ADD PAYMENT TERMS”.

    For example, the course price is € 2,000 x19% VAT = € 380

    Total Payment with Vat is € 2,380

    € 2,000 + € 380 VAT = € 2,380
