# Creating Lightning Lessons on Maven

<!-- sop-section-start: summary -->
## Summary

- Purpose: Create and publish a Maven Lightning Lesson from the provided lesson information.
- Outcome: A Maven Lightning Lesson is created with the right title, schedule, description, speaker, and image.
- Trigger: Alexey provides information for a new Lightning Lesson.
- Frequency: Per Maven Lightning Lesson.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

<!-- sop-section-start: prerequisites -->
## Prerequisites


- Access: Maven school dashboard, ChatGPT or Gemini, DataTalks.Club schedule and calendar.
- Tools: Maven, ChatGPT or Gemini, Google Calendar, Google Sheets.
- Inputs: Lesson details from Alexey, Maven prompt, date and time, speaker information.
<!-- sop-section-end -->
