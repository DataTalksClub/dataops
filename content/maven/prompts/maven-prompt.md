## Procedure

Create publish-ready content using the following structure:

1.  Lesson title in Title Case, clear and benefit -driven.

2.  Three learning outcomes. For each outcome:

- Outcome title (short, specific, max ~60 characters)

- Outcome description explaining how students will understand and apply it (max ~120 characters)

3.  A “why this topic matters” section (max ~400 characters) explaining:

- Why student should care

- The professional or real-world benefit

- How they can expect to grow from this lesson

Tone: practical, concise, professional, outcome-oriented, make it more concrete and less generic and avoid using “strategic
