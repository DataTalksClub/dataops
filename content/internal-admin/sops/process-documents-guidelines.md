### Process Document Guidelines

<!-- sop-step-start id=1 -->
1.  Carefully select the category to which the process falls under.
<!-- sop-step-end -->

<!-- sop-step-start id=2 -->
2.  Always add Context as the introduction to every Process Document.

    Note: Do not just repeat the title of the document, explain the significance of the process or task.
<!-- sop-step-end -->

<!-- sop-step-start id=3 -->
3.  Make sure that the Page Setup is “Pageless”.
<!-- sop-step-end -->

<!-- sop-step-start id=4 -->
4.  In taking screenshots, always consider the visibility of the pictures to highlight the specific step.
<!-- sop-step-end -->

<!-- sop-step-start id=5 -->
5.  In inserting the loom link at the last part, make sure it is clickable.
<!-- sop-step-end -->

<!-- sop-step-start id=6 -->
6.  Do not forget to add every new process document to the “Document Index” with its corresponding link.

    Document Index: [https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1glKmm-NxpHUrHMyyXqN6i4gcUQnobtfIMJmjeiSdcd4/edit?usp=sharing](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1glKmm-NxpHUrHMyyXqN6i4gcUQnobtfIMJmjeiSdcd4/edit?usp=sharing)
