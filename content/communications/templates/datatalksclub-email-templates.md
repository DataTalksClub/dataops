Webinar asking for the title, outline, and others

Also, we need some information from the speaker/s:

- The title of the talk

- The subtitle (or a super-short summary)

- The outline - 3-4 bullet points of what you want to cover

- Bio of the speaker/s

- Links you want to add in the description (LinkedIn profile, Github, Website, etc...)
