# Copy the correct YouTube link for the podcast form

<!-- sop-section-start: summary -->
## Summary

- Purpose: Copy the correct YouTube information needed for the podcast form.
- Outcome: The podcast form can use the correct YouTube link and related description links.
- Trigger: A podcast episode form needs YouTube details.
- Frequency: Per podcast episode form submission.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

<!-- sop-section-start: prerequisites -->
## Prerequisites


- Access: YouTube Studio for the target video.
- Tools: YouTube Studio.
- Inputs: Target podcast video and links from the video description.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

<!-- sop-section-start: procedure -->
## Procedure

<!-- sop-prose-start -->
How to copy the correct YouTube link
This procedure will show you the steps on how to copy the correct YouTube link.

Step-by-step Instructions
<!-- sop-prose-end -->
