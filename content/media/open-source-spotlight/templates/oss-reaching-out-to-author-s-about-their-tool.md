## Template

When to use: Reaching out to OSS Authors about their tool when they don’t know about our event

Subject: \<Tool\> at Open Source Spotlight

Body:

Hi \< NAME \>

I’m (Your Name) and I’m the Community Manager of [DataTalks.Club](http://datatalks.club/). We invite open-source authors to give a demo of their tools. I saw you contribute to \< TOOL\> Would you be interested in giving a demo?

Here’s more information about it:

https://alexeygrigorev.notion.site/Open-Source-Spotlight-bc9c30f79c6b4075bc2cea857560c8f9

Let me know if it’s interesting for you.

Best,

(Your Name)

When to use: We have already talked to the authors about Open to in Social Media

Subject: Demo of \<Tool\> at DataTalks.Club

Body:

Hi \<NAME OF THE AUTHOR\>

As mentioned in our chat, we’re interested to have the demo of your tool in our community.

You can book a slot for the demo with Alexey: https://calendly.com/dtc-alexey/oss

Looking forward to it!

Cheers,
(Your Name)

When to use: From Recommendation

Subject: Demo of \<Tool\> at DataTalks.Club

Body:

Hi \< NAME \>

Nice to meet you! I'm (Your Name), Community Manager of [DataTalks.Club](http://datatalks.club/). We invite open-source authors to give a demo of their tools and \<NAME OF RECOMMENDER\> recommended me to reach out to you to do the demo of your \< TOOL \>

You can read about the Open-Source spotlight [here](https://alexeygrigorev.notion.site/Open-Source-Spotlight-bc9c30f79c6b4075bc2cea857560c8f9).

Let me know if it's interesting for you.

Best,

(Your Name)
