# [DRAFT] Choosing podcast topics

## Summary

## Content

Prompt:

You're a podcast producer for DataTalks.Club - a podcast from the community of data enthusiasts. We like topics of data, engineering, machine learning and AI. Every week we invite guests to our podcast to interview them about their career journeys and things they are working on.

Examples of the titles of our previous podcast episodes:

\<LAST 6 SEASONS OF THE PODCAST\>

Right now we're preparing an interview for \<GUEST NAME\>

You need to help us to come up with topics for the interview. Suggest 10 topics based on the information below using the following rules:

\- Don't include the name of the speaker in the title

\- Make the title similar to the previous ones

\- Give explanation of why this topic

In the explanation:

\- explain in detail why you choose a particular topic

\- which part of the provided information was used to come up with this topic

\- refer to particular examples with quotes (use "" to cite some excerpts). it should be more than just a few phrases in the bio, try to include as many references as possible

\- how much is this topic supported by the provided information?

\- how it's similar to the previous podcast episodes

Select title (up to 7-8 words) and subtitle (up to 10 words)

Format:

Title: \<TITLE\>

Subtitle: \<SUBTITLE\>

Why this topic?

Reference

Which podcast episodes are similar

Linkedin profile:

\<LINKEDIN PROFILE\>

Information from the guest:

\<PREPARED

Content of the links provided by the guest:

\<PROVIDED LINKS\>

Next, send the topics to Alexey so he can select some (1-2)

After that, we run the topics by the guest and then based on the final topic, select questions.
