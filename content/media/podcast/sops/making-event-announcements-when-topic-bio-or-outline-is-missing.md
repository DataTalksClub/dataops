# Making event announcements when topic, bio, or outline is missing

<!-- sop-section-start: summary -->
## Summary

- Purpose: Announce a podcast event even when the topic, bio, or outline is incomplete.
- Outcome: The event is published with placeholders or available guest information.
- Trigger: A podcast event needs announcement before all details are ready.
- Frequency: As needed for podcast announcements.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

<!-- sop-section-start: prerequisites -->
## Prerequisites


- Access: Luma and event announcement templates.
- Tools: Luma, Google Docs templates.
- Inputs: Event title, date and time, available topic, bio, outline, and placeholder text.
<!-- sop-section-end -->

<!-- sop-section-start: procedure -->
## Procedure

<!-- sop-prose-start -->
Making event announcements when topic, bio, or outline is missing

This procedure will show you the steps on how to make a podcast event announcement if the outline is not ready, only the topic. This usually happens when the guest submitted the information (outline and bio) on the podcast document late.

Step-by-step Instructions
In Luma, follow the normal event creation process. If some information is missing, use the guide below:
<!-- sop-prose-end -->

<!-- sop-step-start id=1 -->
1.  If the Outline is missing: Enter "TBA" under the Outline: section.
<!-- sop-step-end -->

<!-- sop-step-start id=2 -->
2.  If the Speaker Bio is missing:

    - Step 1: Check their LinkedIn "About" or "Description" section and use that as the bio.

    - Step 2: If no LinkedIn description exists, use Gemini or ChatGPT to generate a 2-3 sentence bio based on their current job title, company, and past experience.

    - Final Notification Step: Once the announcement is live, notify the guest or sponsor immediately.

    Example:

    Hi Elizaveta and Ernst,

    Hope you are both doing great!

    We have announced the event as we want to start promoting it as early as possible. Let us know when you have the bio and we will update it in the description.

    Here is the link: [https://luma.com/f6zc5lr6](https://luma.com/f6zc5lr6)

    Thank you!
<!-- sop-step-end -->
